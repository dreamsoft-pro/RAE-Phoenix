Description of the promotion

Promotions work for both not logged in clients and logged-in users.
In the case of a logged in client, the priority of a better price for the customer is always taken into account.
When, apart from the promotion, the customer is assigned to a rebate group, or if he uses a rebate coupon, the price that is more advantageous to the customer is always taken into account.
Individual types of price cuts do not add up.

Promotions can be set as:

switched on manually (without setting the promotion start date, require manual shutdown)
time (turned on for date and time, lasting a certain number of days or hours)
repetitive (in as above, we set the cycle in which the promotion is to be repeated in days or hours)

offer

Several software properties

The software enables the calculation of values, presentation and sale of products realized with various printing technologies based on several dozen methods of calculating the price.

Several technologies can be combined in one product, eg digital printing and offset printing, and the program will choose the most favorable price for the customer based on the given parameters.

The configuration for the product can also be set so that the customer can choose what technology his work is to be implemented.

Customizable offer for the end user

The program has a built-in mechanism of rebate groups that allows a percentage reduction of prices of individual products.

A unique feature is the ability to set the value of each process according to individual arrangements with the client.

The mechanism of rebate or process groups works only for logged in users, it also allows to expand the product offer available to the logged user, different for individual clients.

The imposition of the above-mentioned mechanisms, eg promotions or discount codes, always works in the priority of the best price for the customer. Individual types of price cuts do not add up.
